How to schedule a Turnkey Hangouts on Air:

  1. Click the button below to visit the Hangout On Air Page to schedule an event.
  2. Click "Create a Hangout On Air"
  3. In the field "Give it a name", type "{{modalContent.name}}"
  4. In the field "Tell people what it's about", type "{{modalContent.description}}"
  5. Select the tab "Later"
  6. Input date and time "{{modalContent.date | date: 'shortDate'}} {{modalContent.date | date: 'shortTime'}}"
  7. Click Share
